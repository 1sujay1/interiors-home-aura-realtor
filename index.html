Frequently Asked Questions

What makes North Bangalore a good location for real estate investment?

North Bangalore has emerged as one of the most promising real estate destinations due to several key factors:

Strategic Location

Proximity to the international airport, major IT hubs, and excellent connectivity through the Outer Ring Road and Metro network make it highly desirable.

Infrastructure Development

Ongoing and planned infrastructure projects including metro extensions, road widening, and commercial developments are enhancing the area's appeal.

Appreciation Potential

Consistent price appreciation and growing demand from both end-users and investors make it a sound investment choice.

What are the advantages of buying a row house over an apartment?

Row houses offer several advantages that make them an attractive option for homebuyers:

More Space

Enjoy larger living areas, private gardens, and often multiple floors compared to apartments.

Privacy

Benefit from independent living with no shared walls on the sides, offering more privacy than apartments.

Customization

Greater flexibility in terms of interior modifications and exterior personalization.

Better ROI

Typically offer better long-term appreciation compared to apartments in the same area.

What amenities should I look for in a gated community?

When choosing a gated community, consider these essential amenities:

Security

24/7 security personnel, CCTV surveillance, and access control systems.

Recreational Facilities

Clubhouse, swimming pool, children's play area, and landscaped gardens.

Utilities

Power backup, water supply, and waste management systems.

Community Spaces

Walking/jogging tracks, community halls, and open spaces for social gatherings.

What is the current market trend for real estate in North Bangalore?

The North Bangalore real estate market is currently showing several positive trends:

Price Appreciation

Steady price growth in key areas like Thanisandra, Hebbal, and Yelahanka.

Increased Demand

Growing interest from IT professionals and families looking for premium properties.

Infrastructure Boost

New metro lines and road projects are enhancing connectivity and boosting property values.

Rental Market

Strong rental demand, especially in areas close to tech parks and business districts.

What are the key legal documents to verify before buying a property?

Ensure you verify these essential documents before making a purchase:

Title Deed

Verify the ownership and title clearance of the property.

Approved Plans

Check for BBMP/BDA approval and sanctioned building plans.

Encumbrance Certificate

Ensures the property is free from legal dues and loans.

Khata Certificate & Extract

Important for property tax assessment and municipal records.

Occupancy Certificate

Issued by the local authority, certifying the building is ready for occupation.
